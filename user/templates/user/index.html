About the gaming site

We provide seamless and cashless policy to all our customers offline without needing your internet connection when making payment in any of our gaming brand ( no cash or transfer is allow in any our gaming branch ).

How does it work?

1. Register for a new account and if you're existing account holder login into your account if you're first timer.

2. Generate your QR code and create a passcode you wish too to secure your QR code.

3. Fund your account with any amount of your choice by clicking on fund my Acct and follow the steps that follows.

4. You can use your QR code offline in of our gaming branches without internet connection

How can I use my QR code offline?

Visit any of our gaming branch and show our staff your QR code for scanning, you will input your pin to let us know you’re the real owner.

What if my QR code is compromised?

Yes you can by login into your gaming app and click on Qr then reset your QR-Code which automatically deactivates any existing ones.

Do I get refund when someone else use my QR code without my concent?

We are not responsible for that , it’s advisable to keep your QR code safe and not share your password with friends .

If I need help regarding my funding or something else what should I do?

Contact our support center by clicking on CONTACT US button at the top of the site for further assistance.
